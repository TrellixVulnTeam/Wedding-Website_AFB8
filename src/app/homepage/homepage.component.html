Our Story

In 2012 we met when attending Friendship Baptist Church and Vilonia High School. We had gone on a summer church trip to a Texas Travelers game and became friends. After several weeks of hanging out between classes, at lunch, and at church our friendship grew stronger and our relationship evolved. After weeks of Tyler's cheesy jokes and passing hallway notes he gave me a note saying check yes or no. He claims I waited a 4 long hours before finally saying yes. We started dating October 2, 2012. After five amazing years of dating, and growing as a couple he got down on one knee at Garvan Gardens and I said YES! It was one of the most beautiful nights ever!
